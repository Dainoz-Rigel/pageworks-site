This is for

  • • Nigerian digital marketing course creators with a defined curriculum
  • • Coaches and educators already driving traffic or leads
  • • Course owners who want clearer positioning, not louder copy

This is not for

  • • Course ideas still being figured out
  • • General brand writing or content-only engagements
  • • Funnels built on urgency tactics or inflated promises

What usually goes wrong

  • • The page explains modules but not what the buyer gets out of them
  • • Copy is written without considering how visitors scan and scroll
  • • Claims are stacked without structure, proof, or prioritization
  • • The offer feels obvious to the creator and unclear to everyone else

How the engagement works

  1. Audit: I review your current landing page or draft for clarity, structure, and decision-blocking gaps.
  2. Direction: You get direct guidance on what to remove, simplify, or reframe—and why.
  3. Execution: If we proceed, I write and build the page with copy and layout working as one.
